Work that shipped.

Medical Device

A compact UVC eye-treatment device integrating a microcontroller, display, and camera to precisely control exposure while ensuring patient safety. Strict power monitoring and feedback systems maintain regulatory compliance, enabling accurate, repeatable disinfection cycles in a low-power design suitable for clinical and home use.

02 IoT Agricultural Monitoring
RF / Connectivity

A low-power tree health monitoring system using vibration sensing to capture changes in trunk resonance and structural integrity. Data is transmitted via LoRa to a central gateway, enabling scalable, long-range deployment with multi-year battery operation and robust hardware for outdoor environments.

DisciplineRF Design, Multi-layer PCB
ProtocolsWi-Fi 6, BT 5.2, LoRa
Form FactorWaterproof, 5-year deployment
03 Consumer Focus Timer
Consumer Electronics

A compact focus timer that visualises elapsed time through a circular RGB LED array. Aggressive sleep modes and efficient LED driving achieve extended battery life. The hardware and firmware were optimised for low-cost, high-volume manufacture with a minimal component count supporting scalable production.

DisciplineLow-power, Battery, Cost reduction
ConnectivityBluetooth LE 5.1, 2.4 GHz
